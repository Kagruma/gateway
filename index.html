Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto City is the capital city of the Kyoto prefecture in Japan. With a population of 1.47 million, this city is a tourist friendly location. Considered the cultural capital of japan, it's pride lies in the Buddhist temples, palaces and gardens.

Kyoto is one of few Japanese cities that have countless prewar buildings, all of which are attractions available to visit.

The climate of Kyoto

Summers are hot and humid, however, winters are cold with periodical snowfall. Kyoto's rainy season begins around the middle of June and lasts until the end of July.
